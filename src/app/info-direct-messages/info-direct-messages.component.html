Understanding Direct Messages



Who can use this feature?

  • All members and guests
  • Available for all plans

    • What you should know

  • Direct messages can occur between two individuals or with up to nine people.
  • Starting and sending direct messages works the same as composing any other message in EchoSphere. Learn more about how you can send and read messages.
  • If your company uses EchoSphere Connect, you have the ability to send direct messages to individuals in other companies.

    • Tip:
    In EchoSphere, you can quickly start a new direct message at any time by clicking or tapping on the 'Messages' button.
