Customize notification triggers

By default, Slack only accepts direct messages (DMs), Mentions and specific keywords used for notifications. However, you have the option to adjust your personal settings, to either receive all new messages in conversations, in which you are involved, to be notified or not to be notified at all Receive notifications.
